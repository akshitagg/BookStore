All About Your Favorite Books!

           The Alchemist

The Alchemist is a classic novel in which a boy named Santiago embarks on a journey seeking treasure in the Egyptian pyramids after having a recurring dream about it and on the way meets mentors, falls in love, and most importantly, learns the true importance of who he is and how to improve himself and focus on what really matters in life.

The constant theme in The Alchemist is to pursue your dreams by following what your heart desires. During the young boy's journey, he learns to listen to the heart and to follow the language of omens. With each passing obstacle and hurdle that the young boy encounters, there is a lesson to learn.

           The Mountain Is You

The Mountain Is You is a self-discovery book that aims to help its readers tap into their own power and discover their potential by overcoming trauma, life’s challenges, and working on their emotional damages, all through accepting change, envisioning a prosperous future, and stopping the self-sabotage.

Some of the lessons this book teaches us:
Lesson 1: When we realize what our core needs are, we’ll understand why we make certain decisions in life.
Lesson 2: Seek risky opportunities, instead of accepting comfortable life situations.
Lesson 3: Learn to differentiate between intuition and intrusive thoughts.
